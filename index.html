About Buttercup

Buttercup is a fictional character. A member of the Powerpuff Girls together with bubbles and blossom.Buttercup defines herself as a "tough hotheaded tomboy." Her personality ingredient is "spice", her signature color is green, and she has short black hair in a flip. She enjoys getting dirty, fighting hard, and playing rough; she does not plan ahead of time and is all action.
